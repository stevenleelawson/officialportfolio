Origin story:

Obsessed with design from an early age, I got into web development to support my love of a well-designed user interface with the ability to create living and breathing code to produce top notch user experiences. I call myself a Creative Technologist, because I include that passion, combined with my background as an artist, in my projects to create a uniquely personal experience for the user.

Having spent the last 6 months with an exciting start up, I have had the opportunity to hone my wireframing skills, website design and deployment, Adobe Photoshop CC, Adobe Illustrator CC, Adobe InDesign, HTML5, and CSS3.
